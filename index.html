About Me

We can make it together

I am a UX/UI designer, researcher, and design strategist specializing in user-centered design, usability analysis, and interaction design for Fintech, Web3, and Logistics applications. I translate complex systems into intuitive digital experiences through a methodical approach grounded in heuristic evaluation, information architecture, and interaction modeling.

Proficient in tools like Figma, Sketch, Adobe Creative Suite, and prototyping and research platforms, I craft high-fidelity design systems and interactive prototypes. My work bridges design and functionality, ensuring seamless collaboration between cross-functional teams and delivering scalable, accessible solutions.
