About Me

We can make it together

I am a usability designer, researcher and analyst with 3+ years of experience in the field of user experience (UX) and user-centered design (UCD). I help companies to excel and boost its identity, services and applications in the digital phase.

My expertise in design tools such as Sketch, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe illustrator and Figma enables me to create high-fidelity designs that effectively communicate design concepts to stakeholders.
