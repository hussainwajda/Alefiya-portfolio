Amaia

AMAIA (Asset Manager Artificial Intelligence Application) is a financial asset management system powered by artificial intelligence, developed by Creed & Bear. Designed to provide a unique and personalized investment experience, AMAIA allows users to:

• Investment in Algorithms: Users can invest in a variety of algorithms available on the platform.

• Market Behavior Visualization: Users can view the performance and behavior of their algorithms in real-time.

• Fund Management: Users have the ability to invest amounts, make withdrawals, and redistribute funds among different algorithms.

• Detailed Algorithm Information: Users can access comprehensive and detailed information about each algorithm available on the platform.
